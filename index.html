What We Handle

When procurement fails,
it fails at the handoff.

We manage every stage — from supplier identification to customs clearance at destination — so no handoff is unowned and no gap is left to chance.

Discuss a Requirement
SVC-01

Supplier Sourcing & Verification

We identify, qualify, and continuously monitor suppliers across Southeast Asia, China, Turkey, and Eastern Europe. Every supplier undergoes documented financial, compliance, and capacity screening before a purchase order is issued.

  • Factory audit and capacity verification
  • Financial and sanctions screening
  • Compliance documentation review
  • Pre-production sample coordination
SVC-02

FCL / LCL Freight Coordination

Ocean, air, and multimodal freight coordinated to your contracted delivery window — not the carrier's convenience. We book FCL and LCL freight, manage incoterm structuring, and maintain active cargo visibility throughout transit.

  • FCL and LCL ocean freight booking
  • Air freight for time-critical shipments
  • Incoterm selection and documentation
  • Real-time cargo tracking and reporting
SVC-03

Procurement & PO Management

Purchase orders issued, tracked, and managed on your behalf — in the supplier's language, in their timezone, against your specification. We handle price negotiation, production monitoring, and milestone reporting throughout manufacture.

  • PO issuance and supplier communication
  • Price and payment term negotiation
  • Production milestone tracking
  • Pre-shipment quality inspection coordination
SVC-04

Customs Clearance & Trade Compliance

Documentation prepared and verified before goods leave origin — not reviewed after they arrive at port. We manage HS code classification, import licensing, sanctions screening, and country-of-origin certification on every shipment.

  • HS code classification and tariff review
  • Import and export licensing management
  • AML and sanctions screening
  • Certificate of origin and customs documentation
SVC-05

Warehousing & Cargo Consolidation

Bonded warehouse access, cargo consolidation, and cross-docking coordination across primary hubs in Asia, Turkey, and the UAE. We manage consolidation to reduce freight cost without compromising delivery timelines.

  • LCL consolidation and deconsolidation
  • Bonded and free zone warehouse access
  • Cross-docking and transshipment coordination
  • Hub-to-hub distribution management
SVC-06

Trade Lane & Corridor Strategy

Corridor analysis, FTA utilisation, duty optimisation, and supply chain risk mapping for importers entering new sourcing regions or restructuring existing trade lanes. Strategy grounded in active operational knowledge — not desk research.

  • Trade corridor and lead time analysis
  • FTA and preferential duty utilisation
  • Supply chain risk and redundancy mapping
  • Alternative sourcing region identification
How We Operate

Six stages.
One responsible
party.

Every engagement follows a documented methodology. You know what happens at each stage, who owns it, and what the resolution path is when something doesn't go to plan.

Begin an Engagement
01 01

Intake & Scope Definition

Structured intake covering commodity specification, origin preference, destination, target volume, incoterm requirements, and delivery window. No generic proposals — scope is written to your specific requirement before any work begins.

02 02

Supplier Qualification

Shortlisted against your specification from our active supplier network across 40+ countries. Qualification includes capacity verification, financial screening, compliance review, and initial sample coordination. We flag concerns before you commit — not after production starts.

Discrepancies flagged at qualification — not at inspection
03 03

Commercial Negotiation

Price, payment terms, incoterms, and penalty structures negotiated on your behalf — in the supplier's language, in their timezone, with knowledge of local commercial norms and leverage points. Agreed terms are documented and counter-signed before PO issuance.

04 04

Order Execution & Production Oversight

Purchase orders issued and production monitored against agreed milestones. You receive structured status reports — not informal updates. Pre-shipment inspection is coordinated and completed before goods are released for freight booking.

Inspection before release — not after arrival
05 05

Freight Booking & Customs Clearance

Freight booked to your contracted delivery window. All customs documentation prepared and reviewed for compliance before goods depart origin. We manage clearance at both origin and destination — including HS classification, import licensing, and certificate of origin where required.

06 06

Delivery Confirmation & Close-Out

Final delivery confirmed and documented. All shipping records, inspection reports, customs declarations, and commercial documents archived in a complete audit trail. Any discrepancy at delivery is escalated, documented, and resolved through a defined process — not left with the client to manage.

Full audit trail archived on close
