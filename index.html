Destination Markets

Where Pakistani textiles already travel

These six markets are the established import lanes for Pakistani cotton home textiles and apparel — meaning the documentation, compliance norms, and shipping routes are already well-tested for buyers based there.

🇺🇸~17%

United States

The single largest destination for Pakistani exports — deep, established demand for cotton home textiles, towels, and bedding.

🇬🇧~7%

United Kingdom

A long-standing trade relationship with Pakistan, with well-understood import documentation and retail demand for home textiles.

🇩🇪~5%

Germany

The largest single gateway into the EU, with strong demand for woven and finished textile products.

🇳🇱~5%

Netherlands

A major European distribution and re-export hub — goods landing here often move on across the EU.

🇪🇸~4%

Spain

Home to fast-fashion supply chains that source cotton apparel and home textiles at scale.

🇦🇪~5%

UAE

A regional trade and re-export hub for the Gulf, with free-zone logistics that simplify onward distribution.

Figures are approximate shares of Pakistan's total exports by destination (Pakistan Bureau of Statistics / World Bank WITS bilateral trade data, most recent reporting year). These six markets are independently confirmed by WITS as the leading destinations for Pakistani textile and apparel shipments specifically.
