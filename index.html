How It Works

A structured process.
No ambiguity.

Every engagement follows a documented methodology. You know what happens, when it happens, and who is responsible.

Begin an Engagement
01

Discovery & Scope Definition

We begin with a structured intake — commodity type, origin preference, destination, volume, timeline, and compliance requirements. No generic proposals. Every engagement is scoped specifically.

02

Supplier Identification & Qualification

Our network spans vetted suppliers across 40+ countries. We shortlist against your specification and conduct initial qualification — capacity, compliance, and commercial viability.

03

Commercial Negotiation

We negotiate price, terms, incoterms, and payment structure on your behalf — in the supplier's language, in their timezone, with knowledge of local commercial norms.

04

Order Execution & Monitoring

Purchase orders are issued, production is monitored against milestones, and quality inspections are coordinated before shipment. You receive structured status reports throughout.

05

Freight & Customs Coordination

Shipping is booked, documentation is prepared and verified, customs clearance is managed at origin and destination. Nothing is handed to you incomplete.

06

Delivery & Close-Out

Final delivery confirmation, document archiving, discrepancy resolution, and post-engagement debrief. Every shipment closes with a complete audit trail.
